## Topic-Specific Hashtag Construction

Generate 2–3 topic-specific hashtags per video using these rules:
- Pull from the video's primary keyword and most specific supporting keyword
- Use terms the audience actually searches (see audience search language above)
- Specific beats generic: `#nimitzencounter` beats `#mystery`, `#uapdisclosure` beats `#conspiracy`
- No hashtag over 20 characters
- All lowercase, no spaces within hashtag

### Example hashtag sets by topic type:
- Nimitz encounter video: `#strangeuniverse #ufo #nimitzencounter #uap #navyufo`
- Government program video: `#strangeuniverse #ufo #aatip #uapdisclosure #pentagon`
- Historical case video: `#strangeuniverse #ufo #rendlesham #militaryufo #declassified`
- Whistleblower video: `#strangeuniverse #ufo #whistleblower #uaphearing #disclosure`

## Above-the-Fold Guidance

The first 2–3 lines of the description are the highest-value real estate. For Strange Universe, these should:
- Lead with the case name or subject matter, embedding the primary keyword naturally
- Second line: what the viewer will understand after watching — frame it as investigation results
- Third line (optional): a specific detail that signals depth and credibility (e.g., "Including testimony from three naval officers who were ordered to stay silent.")

Example:
```
The Nimitz UFO encounter remains the most well-documented UAP case in military history.
In this investigation, we walk through every piece of evidence — radar data, FLIR footage, and pilot testimony — to understand what the USS Princeton tracked in November 2004.
Commander David Fravor's account is just the beginning.
```

## Shorts Description Rules

For short-form videos:
- Above the fold: 1–2 lines only — lead with the most specific, searchable detail from the short
- No chapter markers
- CTA: 1 line max — pointer to long video if Mode A teaser (e.g., "Full investigation linked above.")
- Hashtags: same rule — 2 fixed + 2–3 topic-specific, 5 max
- Total description: keep under 200 characters where possible
